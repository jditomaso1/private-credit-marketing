Our Mission

Private Credit AI was founded to help credit professionals make faster, smarter decisions. We believe the future of private credit lies in combining domain expertise with advanced technology. Our mission is to enable investment professionals, allocators, and asset managers to scale with precision through AI-powered document parsing, credit risk scoring, and portfolio optimization.

Why We Exist

Private markets are growing faster than traditional systems can support. Our platform bridges the gap—offering rapid ingestion of complex deal materials, consistent underwriting logic, and powerful compliance monitoring. We help clients stay ahead by transforming static documents into dynamic insights.

Who We Serve

We work with a wide range of institutions, including direct lenders, private credit funds, BDCs, CLO managers, family offices, and institutional allocators. Whether you're a seasoned portfolio manager or an emerging platform, our tools are designed to help you move with speed and clarity.

Leadership

Founded by a former investment banker and fintech entrepreneur, Private Credit AI combines deep industry knowledge with proven execution in AI-enabled software. We've lived the pain of fragmented deal processes and built a solution we wished we had inside the industry.
